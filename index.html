Best Books to Read

By Vasundra S

Harry Potter

Written by J.K. Rowling

Age group: 8+

Harry Potter is a really good book for all ages, full of fantasy and magic. It has come as movies as well, but it's always better to read the books first.

Summary

A young boy, Harry Potter is stuck with the Dursleys because His parents were killed by Voldemort. Suddenly, letters keeps coming for him from Hogwarts. The Dursleys, didn't want this, go to an island far away, but Hagrid, a giant, comes and gives him the letter to go to Hogwarts, the school of wizardry and witchcraft, he soon finds that one of the teachers from his school is Voldemort in disgiuse...

Diary of a Wimpy Kid

Written by Jeff Kinney

Age group: 3+

Diary of a Wimpy Kid is a hilarious story full of Greg and has his problems. This series contains 19 books from which 4 have come out as movies.

Summary

A young boy, Greg Heffley is having his first year in Middle School, where students like him have to join elder, meaner and bigger kids. As the year moves on. he records his troubles in his diary.

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

Written by Roald Dahl

Age group: 7+

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory is a book full of Willy Wonka and Charlie's adventure in the Chocolate Factory. It is also made into a movie.

Summary

A young boy, Charlie desperately wants to go to Willy Wonka's Chocolate Factory , where the best chocolate is made. Charlie's luck strikes and he gets to go to the great Chocolate Factory ...

Matilda

Written by Roald Dahl

Age group: 9+

Matilda is the story of an extra-ordinary girl with Telekinetik powers

Summary

A young girl, Matilda is treated like a scab by her parents. When she goes to school, she finds a horrible headmistress, Miss. Trunchbull , who hates children as well as her lovely teacher, Ms.Honey. Matilda takes her revenge with her unusual Telekinetik powers and defeats the Trunchbull for once and for all.
